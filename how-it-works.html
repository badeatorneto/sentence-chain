How It Works

Get started with collaborative storytelling in four simple steps

1

Read the Current Story

Visit the homepage and scroll through today's developing story.

2

Write One Creative Sentence

Click "Add Your Sentence" and craft a single sentence (up to 200 characters).

3

Submit and Join the Chain

Enter your username and watch your contribution appear immediately.

4

Return Tomorrow

Each day at midnight, a fresh story begins.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many sentences can I contribute?
One sentence per day per person.
